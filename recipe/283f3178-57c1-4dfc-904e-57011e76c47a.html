Key West Shrimp with Key Lime Mustard Sauce

Tapas USA - United States of America 185 minutes Easy Total servings - 6

Fresh and delicious, can be served chilled or immediately

Ingredients

  • Mayonaise - 1 cup/s
  • Lime juice - 0.5 cup/s
  • Dijon mustard - 3 tablespoon/s (tbsp @14.3g)
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon/s (tsp @4.2g)
  • Tabasco - 1 to season
  • Salt (sea) - 1 pinch/es (@0.3g)
  • Black pepper (ground) - 1 pinch/es (@0.3g)
  • Beer - 2 bottle/s (@750ml)
  • Lemon - 1 unit/s
  • Seafood seasoning - 2 tablespoon/s (tbsp @14.3g)
  • Garlic (chopped) - 1 tablespoon/s (tbsp @14.3g)
  • Cayenne pepper (ground) - 0.5 teaspoon/s (tsp @4.2g)
  • Shrimp (peeled, raw) - 3 pound/s

Tools

  • One small bowl for sauce
  • One medium/ large bowl for shrimp
  • Boiling pot
  • Plastic wrap

Instructions

Sauce

  1. Stir mayonnaise, lime juice, Dijon mustard, sugar, and hot pepper sauce together in a bowl until smooth; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate mustard sauce for at least 2 hours.

Shrimp

  1. Mix beer, lemon juice, seafood seasoning, hot pepper sauce, garlic, cayenne pepper, and black pepper together in a saucepan; bring to a boil and
  2. add shrimp and cook shrimp just until they are bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, about 2 minutes; drain immediately.

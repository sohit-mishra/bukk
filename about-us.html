About us

Buk K is a central hub of passionate cooks and food fanatics building the next generation of community to share and enjoy culinary experiences from around the world. We’re driven by a simple objective: Happy tummy Happy life.

The company

Buk K was founded in 2005 by mother and daugther (Katarina and Kartika) as a mean to share homecook traditional meals with friends and family. Over the time from word of mouth it evolves into the catering service we now know today.

The mission

Buk K committed to:

  • bring the world a taste of authenticity of traditional meal made only from selection of fresh ingredients,
  • share good food and the secret recipes to make them, and
  • connect thousands of family due to their love of culinary
The people

We’ve got big goals and it’s the people at Buk K that make it possible. We take our work, but not ourselves, seriously. We know that great things happen in happy workplaces, which is why we endeavour to make our platform one that is fun, respectful and friendly
